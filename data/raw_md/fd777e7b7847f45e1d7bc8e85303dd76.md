|---|---|

| Intershop Admin Center | The Intershop Admin Center provides basic features designed to help administrators handling the user management and the assignment of access privileges. |
| Intershop Administration Portal | The Intershop Administration Portal allows to manage applications for business users. It is a multi-tenant cloud-based service which is hosted and maintained by Intershop, providing one platform for all customers. |
