The locking [framework](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2163970267/framework) provides a mechanism to prevent certain parallel processes accessing the same [object](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2158101810/business+object)s at the same time to avoid conflicts. For example, it must be impossible to [import](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729497/import) and to [replicate](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2155352933/data+replication) the same [product](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821932/product)s at the same time to avoid inconsistent data in the [database](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729187/database).
