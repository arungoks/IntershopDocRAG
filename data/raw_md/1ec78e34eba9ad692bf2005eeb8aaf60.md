# Managing Site Settings

This section describes how to control some site-specific settings including server groups, secure URL configuration and URL rewriting.

Sales organizations and channels are represented by sites, i.e., their corresponding business applications (like Commerce Management for sales organizations and channels). These sites or applications implement the intended business processes and provide the needed interfaces for administrative and operative tasks.

Intershop Organization Management allows for controlling several site-specific settings, including

-
Server group association

-
URL rewriting

-
Online status

-
HTTPS usage


The site settings available in Intershop Organization Management correspond to the general site preferences controlled via Intershop System Management.
