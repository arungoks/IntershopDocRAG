# Product Sharing Management

This section describes the tasks involved in managing product sharing relations. The product sharing manager includes two sections:

-
Channels

Lists the sharing relations, i.e., the channels with which product data is shared.

-
Sharing Groups

Lists the sharing groups (product data sets to be shared).
