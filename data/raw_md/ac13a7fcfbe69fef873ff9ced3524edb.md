# Searching for Users

-
Click User Management in the main content area on the welcome screen, or in the side navigation.
The
*User Management*overview page opens. Here you can see a list of all users. -
Enter a name or an e-mail address into the search input field.
Note:The user list is narrowed down while typing the search term, displaying the matching search results.The desired user is found and displayed in the search results.
If you have entered a non-existent username or e-mail address, the results list will be empty. In this case, clear your search or click Reset search term to see all users.
