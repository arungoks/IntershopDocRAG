# Apps and Modules

The Admin Center is one of the apps in the Intershop Administration Portal. Each app comes with certain functionalities. The functional components that complement the base functionality of an app are called modules.

The modules of the Admin Center app are *Application management*, *User management* and *Connection management*.

## Intershop Administration Portal

The Intershop Administration Portal allows to manage applications for business users. It is a multi-tenant cloud-based service which is hosted and maintained by Intershop, providing one platform for all customers.
