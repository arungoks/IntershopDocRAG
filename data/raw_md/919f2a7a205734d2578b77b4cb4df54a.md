# Managing Product Approvals

As mentioned in [Product Approval](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/concept_product_approval.html), the product approval functionality involves two access privileges (or roles): product approvers and catalog & product managers. Product approvers are responsible for approving the product data, catalog & product managers edit the product data and request the approval. Both roles (depending on the current approval status) can set products to "Edit" in case the data is incorrect or incomplete.

[Product Completeness](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/topic_product_completeness.html).

Managing the product approval statuses requires the product approval functionality to be enabled. For details, see [Enabling Product Approval](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/task_enabling_product_approval.html).

There are two ways to switch the product approval status: either directly from the product list, or via the product detail view. The behavior differs.
