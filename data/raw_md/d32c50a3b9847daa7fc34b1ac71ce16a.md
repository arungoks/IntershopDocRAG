# Deleting Connections

This section describes how to delete an existing connection. Connections of type ICM can only be edited, but not completely deleted, after they have been created.

-
Click Connection management in the main content area on the welcome screen, or in the sidebar.
The
*Connection management*overview page opens. Here you can see a list of all connections. -
Click the
*Delete*icon () of the connection in question.The*Delete connection*dialogue opens. -
Click the Delete button (or discard this process by clicking the Cancel button).
The dialog closes and a confirmation message appears.
