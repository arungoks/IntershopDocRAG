The Commerce Management (or Commerce management application) is the main business application of the [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management). As the primary business [management tool](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214741039/management+tool) it allows for business-relevant tasks, like product and catalog management, content management, shop application management, promotion and campaign management, etc.
