# Deleting Gift Card/Gift Certificate

To delete a gift card/gift certificate:
-
Select the channel from the context selection box, then select Marketing | Gift Cards & Gift Certificates.
This displays the Gift Cards & Gift Certificates manager showing a list of all gift certificates in the channel.

-
Select the check box for the gift card/gift certificate to delete.
-
Click Delete.
A confirmation box appears to confirm the deletion.

-
Click OK to confirm the deletion.
This removes the gift card/gift certificate from the system.
