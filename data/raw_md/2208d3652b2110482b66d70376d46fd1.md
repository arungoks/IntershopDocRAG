With ICM 7.10.38-LTS we introduce a new Long Term Support (LTS) release. This ICM release is the last LTS release of the ICM 7.10 generation. We plan to deliver bug fixes via this LTS release until the end of support of ICM 7.10.

With the new 7.10.38-LTS release the 7.10.26-LTS will become obsolete. This means after the final 7.10.26.21-LTS no more hotfix releases will be delivered for 7.10.26-LTS.

We strongly recommend our customers and partners who are still using 7.10.26-LTS releases to switch to the latest LTS version (7.10.32-LTS or 7.10.38-LTS).
