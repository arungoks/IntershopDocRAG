# Managing Contact Us Preferences

Intershop Commerce Management allows to manage preferences for e-mails sent via the contact us form in the storefront for a channel. To do so:

-
Select the management context from the context selection box, then select Preferences.
This displays the Preferences overview page.
-
Select Contact Us Preferences.
The detail page is displayed. The following options are available:
Table 1. Contact Us Preferences Preference Description E-mail From Address Defines the sender's e-mail address. E-mail Template The layout of the e-mail is defined by the e-mail template. E-mail To Address Defines the recipient's e-mail address. -
Click Apply to save your settings.
Otherwise, click Reset to discard your changes.
