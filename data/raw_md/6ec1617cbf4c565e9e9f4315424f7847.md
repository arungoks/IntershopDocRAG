[sessionlessurl()](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ENFDEVDOC/pages/1775689606/ISML+Function+-+sessionlessurl)and `sessionlessurlex()`

are used in templates to dynamically generate URLs as part of a link. As opposed to [url()](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ENFDEVDOC/pages/1775690000/ISML+Function+-+url)and [urlex()](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ENFDEVDOC/pages/1775690011/ISML+Function+-+urlex), these functions generate URLs without session IDs.

For details about the function syntax, parameters, etc., refer to [url()](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ENFDEVDOC/pages/1775690000/ISML+Function+-+url)and [urlex()](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ENFDEVDOC/pages/1775690011/ISML+Function+-+urlex).
