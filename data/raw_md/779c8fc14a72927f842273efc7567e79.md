This overview includes documentation about cache management in Intershop Commerce Management.

Cache management refers to the common approach for controlling all types of caches as provided by the cache engine. This includes:

Implementation and registration of caches that are assigned to the cache layers at development time

(Selective) clearing of caches at runtime

Monitoring and configuration of caches at runtime
