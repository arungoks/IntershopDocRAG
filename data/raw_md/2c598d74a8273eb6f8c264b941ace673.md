# Content Management

This section covers tasks related to creating, managing and sharing master pages, components and other assets that are subject to content management.

Note:

Intershop Commerce Management provides a powerful framework that allows for editing storefront pages on different levels of detail, in multiple contexts, and with possibly various functional modifications. The general content structure, however, is defined when setting up the store. Therefore the typical content management use cases are limited to assigning content assets to intended targets and editing actual content like texts or images for both static pages (imprint, FAQ) and "functional" pages (catalog/product views, shopping cart, checkout).
