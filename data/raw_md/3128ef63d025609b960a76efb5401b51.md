# Modifying Customer Data

-
Search for the customer.
For details, see
[Searching Customers](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iom/master/iom_en/topics/managing_customers/topic_searching_customers.html#task_buyer_search). -
In the result list, click on the customer number you want to edit.
This opens the customer detail page.Tip:You can also access the customer details via clicking on the customer number on the order detail page (see
[Searching Orders](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iom/master/iom_en/topics/managing_orders/topic_searching_orders.html#task_order_search)). -
Click Customer Data.
This expands the customer data section.
-
Click Edit.
This displays the editable data in edit boxes or checkboxes, correspondingly.
-
Modify the data as necessary, then click Save.
Otherwise, click Cancel to discard your settings.
