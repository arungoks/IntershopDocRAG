In general, transaction management refers to [feature](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152516365/feature)s for organizing, processing and supporting all shopping-related transactions done by customers in the [storefront](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2178600227/storefront). A shopping cart in an [online shop](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2179410694/online+shop) is a virtual basket where the [customer](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153810357/customer)s collect the [product](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821932/product)s they want to buy. Depending on the [online shop](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2179410694/online+shop), the shopping cart offers different features, like changing the quantity of products, deleting products from the shopping cart, redeeming coupons, starting the [checkout](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153810345/checkout).

In [e-commerce](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821657/e-commerce) the checkout process reflects the steps that have to be performed to place an order. All data needed to fulfill the order have to be provided by the customer.
