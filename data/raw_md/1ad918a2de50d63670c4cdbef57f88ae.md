This guide lists all migrations required from Intershop Order Management 4.6 to 4.7. The document is aimed at project developers.

The database migration from 4.6 to 4.7 does not require the application to be shut down. It is expected to run under 5 minutes.

Version 15 of PostgreSQL client is more strict when it comes to encoding errors in SQL-files. Any content in SQL-files, that is not conform to UTF8 will lead to an error. It is required to fix such problems in custom SQL-files.
