# Ending a Contact Session

After you have resolved the customer's requests, you end the contact session.

To do so, click the End session button of the contact session page.

Ending a contact session returns you to the simple customer search page.

After you have resolved the customer's requests, you end the contact session.

To do so, click the End session button of the contact session page.

Ending a contact session returns you to the simple customer search page.
