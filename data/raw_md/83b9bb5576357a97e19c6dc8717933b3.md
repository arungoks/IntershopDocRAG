# Assigning Pages/Includes to Products

To assign pages or includes to a product:

-
Locate the product you intend to edit.
For details, see
[Locating Products](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/task_locating_products.html). -
Click the name of the product.
The General tab of the Standard Product dialog is displayed.
-
Open the Content tab.
This displays all content views (pages/includes) assigned to this product.
-
In the intended section (Pages/Includes), click New.
This displays the available page types.
-
Select the intended content type and click Apply.
The page/include of the selected type is assigned to the product. You are returned to the Content tab, which displays the updated content list.
