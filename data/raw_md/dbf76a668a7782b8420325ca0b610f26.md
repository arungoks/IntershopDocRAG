Experience management is the collection of processes a [merchant](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2158755994/merchant) uses to track, oversee and organize all interactions with customers during their relationship. With [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management), this includes marketing features like [A/B test](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152515333/A+B+test)s or [promotion](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153813271/promotion)s as well as [web content management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2192548837/web+content+management), [media asset management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214730552/media+asset+management), [SEO](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153813349/SEO) and others.
