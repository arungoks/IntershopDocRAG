# Changing Swatch Image Source

Swatch images of product variations can be maintained either by using the image management or by using custom product attributes. This setting applies on channel level.

To change the swatch image source:

-
Select the channel from the context selection box.
For example inTRONICS Business.
-
Go to Preferences | Product Variations.
The channel preferences page for Product Variations is displayed.
- Select the desired swatch image source and click Apply.
