# Product Assignment

"Product assignment" refers to the (logical) allocation of a product to a catalog category. Via product assigment, products become part of a catalog.

Product assignment can be done either manually or dynamically:

-
Manual assignment

Manual assignment means that the category - product associations are created explicitly when editing a product or a category.

-
Dynamic assignment

Dynamic assignment makes it possible to automate the category-product associations on the basis of product filters, see

[Predefined Product Filters](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/concept_predefined_product_filters.html).Note:Product filters require an integrated third-party search engine, see[Search Index Services](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/operation_maintenance/concept_search_index_services.html).

For details about manual product assignments, see [Managing Products Within Catalog Categories](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/topic_managing_products_within_catalog_categories.html#task_pim_cat_catCategMgmt).

For details about dynamic product assignments, see [Assigning Products Using Filters](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/task_assigning_products_using_filters.html).
