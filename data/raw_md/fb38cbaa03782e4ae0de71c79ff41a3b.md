The Intershop Contact Center is an [OCST](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2215707039/Omni-Channel+Services+Toolset)-based demo [ application](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152517685/application) that is intended to be enhanced by Intershop partners to serve [call center](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153810309/call+center) processes. It already provides basic [feature](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152516365/feature)s designed to help [merchant](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2158755994/merchant)s establish and manage personal contact with their [customer](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153810357/customer)s. It integrates multiple [communication type](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2200667329/communication+type)s (phone, chat, e-mail, etc.) that offer one-to-one contact, and provides customers with a quick and easy way of obtaining information and getting help.
