# Include Management

Content managers use Intershop Commerce Management to edit includes. For details about includes and other content management concepts, see [Content Management: Concepts](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/content_management/topic_content_management_concepts.html).

Note: Includes are development artifacts and thus, Intershop Commerce Management users can only configure includes and assign components to them.
