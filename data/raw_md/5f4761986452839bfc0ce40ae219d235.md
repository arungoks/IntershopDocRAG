# Content Sharing Manager

Using the Content Sharing manager, content assets (page templates and component templates, or pages, page variants, components, includes and content views) can be shared with the corresponding subordinate context, that is, from organizations with channels and from channels with applications. This enables channels or applications, respectively, to reuse, for example, complete page variants or individual components maintained the level above, regardless of any page assignments.
