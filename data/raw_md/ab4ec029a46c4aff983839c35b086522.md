# Enabling/Disabling E-Mail Confirmations for Newsletter Subscriptions

-
Select the channel from the context selection box, then select Marketing | Newsletter.
This displays the Subscriptions tab with a table containing all registered e-mail addresses.
- Go to the Settings tab.
-
Select or unselect the checkbox E-mail Confirmed and click Apply.
Note:Whether the setting E-mail Confirmed is necessary or not, depends on the legal requirements of the respective country. For more information, see
[Newsletter Marketing](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/online_marketing/concept_newsletter_marketing.html).
