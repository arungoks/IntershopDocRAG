Discover the Intershop 7 Data Replication feature. Learn something about how to keep shop information up to date without maintenance downtimes.

Data replication is a special mechanism used to transfer large amounts of data between two Intershop 7 clusters, i.e., from a source to a target system via fast mass-data operations.

The following Video Tutorial presents and explains the feature and shows, how a local Data Replication process is done on a single replication cluster.
