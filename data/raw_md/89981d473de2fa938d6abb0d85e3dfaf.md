# Disconnecting from Intershop Commerce Management

There may be various reasons to disconnect from Intershop Commerce Management (ICM). For example, if you manage several different organizations.

To disconnect from ICM:

Click Connected to: ICM in the header and select Disconnect from ICM.
This will disconnect your Intershop Commerce Management and open the

*Connect to your Intershop Commerce Management* dialog, where you can connect to another Intershop Commerce Management instance, see

[Connecting to Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/getting_started/task_connecting_to_intershop_commerce_management.html).
