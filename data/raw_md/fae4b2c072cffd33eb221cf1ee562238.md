# Apps & Modules

This section provides guidance on managing apps and modules in the Admin Center.

For an explanation of what apps and modules are, see [Apps and Modules](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/iac_en/iac/getting_started/concept_apps_and_modules.html).

Within the *Application management* module, you can find an overview of available apps and modules, and view the app and module details. This module can also serve as starting point for assigning and removing users from apps and modules.
