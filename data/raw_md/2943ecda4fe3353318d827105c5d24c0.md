# Viewing Short Links

To view the details for a short link:
-
Open the Short Links manager.
-
Select the channel from the context selection box, then select Marketing | Link Management.
-
Click Short Links.

-
Click the URL of a short link.
This displays the detail view with the General tab open for the short link.
