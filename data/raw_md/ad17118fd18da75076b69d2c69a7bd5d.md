Intershop Order Management is an [Intershop Commerce Suite](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2213812637/Intershop+Commerce+Suite) [application](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152517685/application) that is designed to combine [order](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153813215/order) processes into one system. It automates and streamlines the life-cycle of [order](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153813215/order)s and payments. Intershop Order Management processes orders from multiple [touchpoint](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2163980932/touchpoint)s ([web shop](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2179410721/web+shop), [mobile shop](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2179410709/mobile+shop), [call center](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153810309/call+center), etc.), allocates them to multiple fulfillment solutions (fulfillment centers, drop-ship distributors, physical stores, etc.), and tracks all order and payment transactions.

Intershop Order Management offers a centralized platform for managing distributed inventory, order, invoice, and payment life cycles, and provides [call center](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153810309/call+center) functionality, enabling real-time visibility into customer's purchasing behavior, stock levels, payments, and more. As part of [Intershop Commerce Suite](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2213812637/Intershop+Commerce+Suite), it utilizes the suite's transaction, PIM, and merchandising [feature](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152516365/feature)s.
