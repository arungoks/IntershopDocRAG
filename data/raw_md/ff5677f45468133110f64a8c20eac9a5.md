# Recommendation Data Feed

Intershop Commerce Management can integrate product recommendation engines as optional components. The corresponding recommendation data feeds can be considered a specific use case of product data feeds, see [Product Data Feeds: Concepts](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/topic_product_data_feeds_concepts.html). Hence, the handling and their behavior are similar.
