# Customer Overview

This section describes the customer overview page.

After you have started a contact session (see [Starting a Contact Session](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/contact_sessions/task_starting_a_contact_session.html)), the Intershop Customer Engagement Center
displays the customer overview page.

The customer overview page is your one-stop location to:

- View the customer's recent orders,
- Check customer data,
- Access the functionality for handling the customer's request, like impersonation, processing carts and orders or generating and applying coupons.

Note:

Clicking on the customer's e-mail address opens a new e-mail in your local e-mail client.
