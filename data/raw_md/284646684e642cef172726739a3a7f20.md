# Page Management

Content managers use Intershop Commerce Management to create new pages and edit existing pages. For details about pages and other content management concepts, see [Content Management: Concepts](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/content_management/topic_content_management_concepts.html).

Pages can be shared from the organization and created, modified, etc. on the channel level or application level.
