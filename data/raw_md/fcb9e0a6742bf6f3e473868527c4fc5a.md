# Channel Management Main Tasks

Main tasks involved in channel management include:

-
Create Channel

Channels can be of different types. Depending on the selected type, different business applications are made available to channel users, see

[Creating Channel](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/operation_maintenance/task_creating_channel.html). -
Manage Channel Access Privileges

In order to perform channel management tasks, channel users need special access privileges. These access privileges can be assigned directly to individual users, to individual department roles or to master roles of the sales or partner organization, see

[Managing Channel Access Privileges](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/operation_maintenance/topic_managing_channel_access_privileges.html#task_ch_mgmt_privil).
