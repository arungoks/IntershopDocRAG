Description | This job is required to support the execution of replication processes on the Live JobServer. To do this, it is necessary to synchronize the live and edit staging processes by copying replication/staging information from the edit system to the live system and to create job configurations on the live system. Created job configurations are configured to be scheduled on the JobServer. |
Scheduling | Scheduled interval is every 10 minutes per default, beginning at 12:00 a.m. |
What Happens Switched Off | Replication processes defined on the edit system will not be started on the live system's JobServer. |
