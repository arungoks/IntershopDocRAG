# Adding Cancellation Confirmation

-
Search for the order.
For details, see
[Searching Orders](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iom/master/iom_en/topics/managing_orders/topic_searching_orders.html#task_order_search). -
In the result list, click on the order number you want to edit.
This opens the order detail page.
-
In the Cancellations tab header, click the symbol.
Otherwise, open the Cancellations tab and click the icon. This opens the Cancellation dialog.
-
Specify the cancellation details as necessary.
- Depending on the origin of the cancellation, switch either to the Cancellation supplier tab or to the Cancellation shop tab.
- Select a return type from the drop-down list.
- Specify a comment.

-
Click Save.
Otherwise, click Cancel to discard your settings.
