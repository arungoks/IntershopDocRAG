In [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management), the REST [framework](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2163970267/framework) serves as the foundation for [RESTful](http://docs.oracle.com/javaee/6/tutorial/doc/gijqy.html) [web service](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160822270/web+services)s [API](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2180158457/API)s. The REST framework acts as an additional access [layer](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2180161411/layer) on top of the application layer. Specific APIs (back office, storefront, integration services, customer-specific applications, etc.) can be defined on top of this REST layer.
