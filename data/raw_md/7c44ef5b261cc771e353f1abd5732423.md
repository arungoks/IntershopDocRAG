# Component Template Management

Content managers use Intershop Commerce Management to create and modify model-based component templates. For details about component templates and other content management concepts, see [Content Management: Concepts](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/content_management/topic_content_management_concepts.html).

Component templates can be shared from the organization and created, modified, etc. on the channel level or application level.
