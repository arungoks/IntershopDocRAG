# Product Data Feeds

Product data feeds are files that contain information about the products listed on your site. They are used to "feed" this information to search engines, product comparison websites, and other similar aggregators of e-commerce information.

Intershop Commerce Management supports multiple product data feed formats, including:

-
Standard Intershop XML

-
Sitemap XML

-
Feed Dynamix Feed

-
Product RSS Feed


For details about product data feeds, supported formats etc., see [Product Data Feeds: Concepts](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/topic_product_data_feeds_concepts.html).
