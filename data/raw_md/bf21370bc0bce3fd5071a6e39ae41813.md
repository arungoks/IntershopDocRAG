# Managing Contact Information

## Viewing Contact History of a Customer

-
Search for the customer.
For details, see
[Searching Customers](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iom/master/iom_en/topics/managing_customers/topic_searching_customers.html#task_buyer_search). -
In the result list, click on the customer number you want to edit.
This opens the customer detail page.
-
Switch to the History tab.
This displays the contact history of the selected customer.

## Adding New Contact Activity

-
Search for the customer.
For details, see
[Searching Customers](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iom/master/iom_en/topics/managing_customers/topic_searching_customers.html#task_buyer_search). -
In the result list, click on the customer number you want to edit.
This opens the customer detail page.
-
Switch to the History tab.
This displays the contact history of the selected customer.
-
Click Add.
This displays an empty contact details dialog.
-
Specify the data as necessary, then click Save.
Otherwise, click Cancel to discard your settings.
