# Deleting Payment Method Configurations

To delete a payment method configuration:
-
Select the channel from the context selection box, then select Orders | Payment Methods.
This displays a list view of all payment methods of the sales channel.

-
Select the checkbox in the row of the payment method you intend to delete.
-
Click Delete, and confirm the operation.
This removes the payment method configuration from the sales channel.
