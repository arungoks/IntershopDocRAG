The organization is a corporate actor in [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management). It can refer to the [sales organization](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153809331/sales+organization) (owner or "root" organization) or a [partner](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821892/partner) and represents their corresponding (top-level) [management context](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2227902772/management+context).

In [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management), the [management tool](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214741039/management+tool) for basic business administration tasks, like [organization](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821796/organization) management, services management, [data replication task](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821649/data+replication+task) management, etc.
