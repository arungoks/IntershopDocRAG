# Changing the Intershop System Management Password

Note:

The default Intershop System Management username is

`admin`

and cannot be changed.-
Click the Change Password button .
The Change Password page is displayed.
- Enter the old password, the new password and confirm the new password in the corresponding fields.
-
Click Apply.
Your password has now been changed.
