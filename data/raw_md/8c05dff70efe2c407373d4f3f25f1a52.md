Search engine optimization is the process of improving the volume or quality of traffic to a web site from [web search engine](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153813361/web+search+engine)s via natural, organic or algorithmic search results. Typically, the earlier a site appears in the search results list, the more visitors it will receive from the web search engine. SEO may target different kinds of search, including image search, local search, and industry-specific vertical search engines.
