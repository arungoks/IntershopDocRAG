# Content Sharing for Partners: Concept

Content assets, which are subject to storefront editing (pages, page variants, includes, components, page templates and component templates), can be shared across channels. With this respect, partner channels can "pass through" content assets from a parent sales or partner organization to a partner. The partner, in turn, shares pages and components to its sales channels.

For details about sharing content assets with partners, see [Sharing Content With Partners](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/operation_maintenance/topic_sharing_content_with_partners.html#task_ptn_share).

For details about sharing content assets with channels (as performed in Intershop Commerce Management for the partner), see [Content Asset Sharing](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/content_management/topic_content_asset_sharing.html).
