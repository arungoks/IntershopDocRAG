# Viewing Apps and Modules

-
Click Apps & modules in the main content area on the welcome screen or in the sidebar.
The
*Apps & modules*overview page opens. Depending on your access privileges, you can see one or more cards representing the individual apps with their modules. Furthermore, each card displays the app's version, a description, and the number of assigned users. - To view the details, click on the upper part of the card for the app's details, or on one of the modules for the module's details.
