# Organization Management: Concepts

Intershop Commerce Management enables you to build a department structure which reflects the internal structure of your organization. Users are related to departments via the assignment of roles. These roles define tasks users can perform, and they ultimately also determine the functional parts of the application which users can access or see when logging in to Intershop Commerce Management.
