This document replaces the outdated article with the ID *248H61 *and the title *Video Tutorial: Intershop 7 Product Sharing & Syndication.*

This Video Tutorial gives an overview about product data transfer features of Intershop 7. Sharing and syndication are explained, compared and their configuration will be presented.

The Intershop 7 product syndication and sharing features allow you to roll out product information in your single sales channels. Product prices and attributes can be copied or modified easily by rules.

Additionally to this tutorial, you can follow the tutorial tasks in the *Guidance* section below the video.
