# Deleting Product Filters

To delete product filters:

-
Select the channel from the context selection box, then select Catalogs | Predefined Product Filters from the navigation bar.
This displays the list of all product filters defined for this channel.
-
Select the product filter(s) to be deleted.
Use the checkboxes to select the product filters you intend to delete.
-
Click Delete, then OK to confirm the deletion.
The selected product filters are removed.
