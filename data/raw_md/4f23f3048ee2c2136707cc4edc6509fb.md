# Taking Over a Cart

While resolving a customer's request, you may need to take over the customer's open cart for further processing.

To do so:

-
Start (if not already running) a contact session, see
[Starting a Contact Session](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/contact_sessions/task_starting_a_contact_session.html). - Switch to the Quick order tab.
-
Click Take over cart.
If there are several open carts, click next to Take over cart, and then select the cart to be opened from the displayed list.
Taking over a cart gives you the control over all items that are currently associated with the cart. You can now proceed to modify the cart items or any other order details.
