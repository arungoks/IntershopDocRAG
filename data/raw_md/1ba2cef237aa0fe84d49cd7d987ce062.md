# Enabling Product Approval

Note:

The tasks described in this section are performed in the context of a sales channel.

This manager allows you to enable the product approval functionality for the current channel, see [Product Approval](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/concept_product_approval.html). To enable product approval:

-
Select the channel from the context selection box, then select Preferences.
This displays the Preferences overview page.
-
Select Product Editing and Product Approval.
The Product Editing and Product Approval details page is displayed.
-
In the Product Approval section, select the checkbox Enable.
To disable the product approval, deselect the checkbox.
-
Click Apply to save your settings.
Otherwise, click Reset to discard your changes.
