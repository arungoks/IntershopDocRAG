# Creating a New Cart

-
Start (if not already running) a contact session, see
[Starting a Contact Session](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/contact_sessions/task_starting_a_contact_session.html).
-
Switch to the Quick order tab.
-
Click .
This displays the *Take over cart* dialog.

-
Click Create a new cart.
The new cart is ready to be filled with products.
