In [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management), campaigns provide a means to combine multiple [promotion](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153813271/promotion)s.

In addition, shop managers can assign dedicated content assets to campaigns as well as define specific target groups and validity periods for campaigns.
