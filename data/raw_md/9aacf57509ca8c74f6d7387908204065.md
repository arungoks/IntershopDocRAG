[Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management) [feature](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152516365/feature) aimed at keeping storefronts clean from profane language. Channel administrators can define words, relative to a locale, that are not allowed in the [channel](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2190215052/channel)'s storefront [application](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152517685/application)s. As a consequence, any use of these words in product reviews, gift card messages or other content that customers can edit is then blocked.
