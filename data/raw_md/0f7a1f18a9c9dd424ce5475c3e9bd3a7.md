# Deleting Classification Catalog

To delete a classification catalog:
-
In the navigation bar, select Classification Catalogs.
A list of all global classification catalogs in the system is displayed.

-
Click Delete on the row of the catalog you intend to delete.
A confirmation box appears prompting you to confirm the deletion.

-
Click OK to confirm the deletion.
The catalog is removed from the system.
