# Managing Product Content Views

Each product can be assigned one or more content views, i.e., pages and/or includes. Generally, pages and includes are subject to content management, see [Content Management: Concepts](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/content_management/topic_content_management_concepts.html). Assignments of pages and includes are controlled by catalog managers using the Content tab of the Standard Product dialog.

The Content tab is divided into two sections:

-
Pages: Lists the product pages assigned to the current product.

-
Includes: Lists the includes assigned to the current product.
