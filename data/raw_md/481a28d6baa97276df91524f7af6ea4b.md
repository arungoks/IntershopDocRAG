The managed service framework is a collection of standardized programming [feature](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152516365/feature)s provided by [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management) to integrate third-party [application](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2152517685/application)s or external services easily via "adapter" [cartridge](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2153817115/cartridge)s. The framework also includes some standard [back office](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2159969605/back+office) functionality for controlling integrated services.
