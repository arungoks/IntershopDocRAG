# Orders Search

Orders can be searched by simple search (using order number, customer, customer type, channel, or order state to filter the result) or attribute-based parametric search (advanced search).

For details, see [Searching Orders](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/orders_fulfillment/topic_searching_orders.html#task_order_mgmt_search).
