# Placing Orders

Once all cart and order details are properly set and applied, you can place the order on the customer's behalf. To do so:

- Switch to the Quick order tab.
-
Click Place order.
In a B2C scenario, this submits the order immediately to Intershop Commerce Management and displays an order summary.In a B2B scenario, this initiates the approval process and displays an information.
