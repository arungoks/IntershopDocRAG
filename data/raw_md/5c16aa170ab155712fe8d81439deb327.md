# Viewing Orders

-
Search for the customer.
For details, see
[Searching Customers](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iom/master/iom_en/topics/managing_customers/topic_searching_customers.html#task_buyer_search). -
In the result list, click on the customer number you want to edit.
This opens the customer detail page and immediately displays the Orders tab.
-
Click on an order number to view the details of an individual order.
This takes you to the order detail page (see
[Order Detail Page Overview](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iom/master/iom_en/topics/managing_orders/topic_order_detail_page_overview.html)).
