# Deleting Product Attachments

To delete a product attachment:

-
Locate the product for which you want to manage attachments.
For details, see
[Locating Products](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/task_locating_products.html). -
Click the name of the product.
The General tab of the Standard Product dialog is displayed.
-
Change to the Attachments tab.
This displays the attachments defined for this product.
- Select the checkbox of the attachment to be deleted.
-
Click Delete, then OK to confirm the deletion.
The selected product attachment group is deleted.

Note:

Deleting a product attachment only unassigns the attachment association. Physically, the (previously attached) file remains unaltered in the content directory.
