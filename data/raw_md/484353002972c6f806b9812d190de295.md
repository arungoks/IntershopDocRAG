# Editing Customer Segment

To edit a custom customer segment:
-
Select the channel from the context selection box, then select Customers | Customer Segments.
A list of all custom customer segments is displayed.

-
Select the name of the customer segment to edit.
The customer segment detail view is displayed.

-
Edit the settings as necessary and click Apply.

Note: Implicit customer segments cannot be deleted.
