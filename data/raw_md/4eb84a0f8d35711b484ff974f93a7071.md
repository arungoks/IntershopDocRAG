# Connecting to Intershop Commerce Management

To use the Intershop Customer Engagement Center and access customer data, store data, and use features such as *Impersonation*, you must provide your Intershop Commerce Management (ICM) credentials.

-
Open the Intershop Customer Engagement Center app.
If there is no connection to Intershop Commerce Management yet, the following dialog opens:
-
Enter the name of your organization.
Note:This step will be skipped if a default organization is configured.
-
Click Continue.
Another dialog opens.
-
Enter your ICM user name and password, or, if configured, log in via single sign-on.
Clicking Connect starts your Intershop Customer Engagement Center session and takes you to the customer search page. For information on how to disconnect from Intershop Commerce Management, see
[Disconnecting from Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/getting_started/task_disconnecting_intershop_commerce_management.html).
