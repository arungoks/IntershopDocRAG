# Viewing Product History

Note:

The product history must be activated. For details about how to activate the product history, see

[Activating Product History](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/icm/master/icm_main_en/catalogs_products/task_activating_product_history.html).To view the product history:

-
Select the intended management context from the context selection box, then select Catalogs | Product History.
This displays the Added Products tab of the Product History.
- Change to the Added Products, Updated Product, or Deleted Products tab.
-
Filter by date or user and click Find.
This displays the specified modifications.
