# Product Completeness

The Product Completeness page shows overall product statistics and provides assistance in identifying and correcting wrong or missing data.

To display this data, catalog and product managers can run product completeness checks. Therefore product completeness rules must be defined and applied to selected product sets. The Product Completeness section then summarizes the produced check results.
