# Getting Started

The Intershop Customer Engagement Center provides basic features designed to help merchants establishing and managing personal contact with their customers. It integrates multiple communication channels that offer one-to-one contact, and provides customers with a quick and easy way of obtaining information and getting help.

-
[Getting Started](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/getting_started/topic_cec_getting_started.html)This section provides general information about the Intershop Customer Engagement Center. Moreover, it covers the topics of access, navigation, and interaction with customers.

[Contact Sessions](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/contact_sessions/topic_contact_sessions.html)This section explains how to search for customers, how to start/end a contact session and storefront impersonation.

[Creating Coupons for Sending to a Customer](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/contact_sessions/task_creating_coupons_for_sending_to_a_customer.html)This section provides information on creating coupons and sending them via e-mail.

[Managing Orders](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iap/master/cec_en/cec/managing_orders/topic_managing_orders.html)This section provides instructions for managing orders.
