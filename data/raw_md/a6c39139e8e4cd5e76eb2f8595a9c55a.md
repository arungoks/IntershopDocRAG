# Deleting Customer Accounts

-
Search for the customer.
For details, see
[Searching Customers](https://knowledge.intershop.com/oh/go.php/iom/master/iom_en/topics/managing_customers/topic_searching_customers.html#task_buyer_search). - Open the customers detail page.
- Click the Delete customer account icon on the upper right corner of the page.
-
Confirm the dialog that asks to delete the customer acccount.
The successful deletion will redirect you to the customer search page.Note:Once the customer account is deleted, the customer detail page is no longer reachable. All business objects such as purchase orders, invoices, payments, shipping, returns, etc. can be processed further if required. Aggregated invoices and customer balance are no longer available.
