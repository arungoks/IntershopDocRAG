The Intershop Copilot for Buyers, an AI-powered purchase and service assistant, is designed to enhance the customer experience by providing a conversational interface for product discovery and service assistance. It leverages advanced Large Language Model AI technology to understand customer intent and deliver precise product recommendations. Integrated with SPARQUE.AI, it ensures accurate search results and personalized suggestions, making the shopping process efficient and user-friendly. The Copilot is seamlessly embedded into the Intershop Progressive Web App, allowing for intuitive interactions with various UI elements. Additionally, it supports voice commands and image-based product recognition, further simplifying the customer journey.

For detailed information on the PWA integration of the Intershop Copilot for Buyers, see [GitHub: intershop-pwa | Guide - Intershop Copilot for Buyers](https://github.com/intershop/intershop-pwa/blob/develop/docs/guides/copilot.md).
