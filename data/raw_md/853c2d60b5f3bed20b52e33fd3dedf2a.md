SPARQUE.AI is an AI-based search and recommendation service. The AI-supported algorithms can be fed from a variety of data sources such as ERP, e-commerce, PIM, but also the browsing history, the device used or real-time data such as the weather. SPARQUE.AI communicates seamlessly with various platforms via an API-first approach.

This document covers the integration of SPARQUE.AI with Intershop Commerce Management.
