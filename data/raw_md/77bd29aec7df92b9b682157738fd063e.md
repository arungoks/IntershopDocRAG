The Intershop Markup Language (ISML) is a template language for rendering dynamic pages, in particular dynamic web pages. [ISML template](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160822230/ISML+template)s are translated by an ISML compiler into JSP templates. ISML provides means to retrieve data from the [pipeline](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821914/pipeline) dictionary, to trigger the execution of pipelines, and to embed [ISML templates](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160822230/ISML+template) inside other ISML templates.
