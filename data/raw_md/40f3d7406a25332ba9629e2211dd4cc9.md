In [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management), import refers to operations for migrating data from external sources into the [database](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729187/database) (based on the [import/export framework](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729501/import+export+framework)).

In [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management), export refers to operations for migrating data out of the [database](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729187/database) for further use in other systems (based on the [import/export framework](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729501)).

The [Intershop Commerce Management](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2217982072/Intershop+Commerce+Management) import/export [framework](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2163970267/framework) consists of specific Java classes, [pipelet](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821909/pipelet)s, and [pipeline](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2160821914/pipeline)s for importing and exporting data to and from the [database](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729187/database). The framework is used by the standard import/export wizards that guide [back-office](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2159969605/back+office) users through standard [import](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729497/import) or [export](https://knowledge.intershop.com/kb/go.php/a/ISGLOS/pages/2214729499/export) operations. Developers can use the framework to extend the functionality or to customize existing import and export operations.
