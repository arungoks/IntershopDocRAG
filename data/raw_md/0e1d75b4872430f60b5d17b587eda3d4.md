The different components of the ICI (ICI, BA, TC, CLT and RMC) are developed independently from each other. Consequently each component has its own sequence of release numbers.

However, there are certain dependencies concerning compatibility between them. In other words not all combinations of versions of components create a valid configuration of the system.

On this page you can find information concerning the release dates of all components and their compatibility among each other.

This information is interesting to anybody who uses TC, CLT or RMC cartridges.
